I have secrets.

Ones I've never said out loud.

Until now.

These are for you.

Only you.

secret one

When I receive a notification,
I always hope it's from you.

secret two

I put my accounts on DND and AFK
when I'm talking to you.

You have my full attention.
Always.

secret three

I love your voice.

But I'm scared to call —
I'm too shy, and I don't want you
to get in trouble because of me.

secret four

I was planning to get
a wristband with your name.

A chain with your name.

So you're always close.

secret five

I pushed every female friend away.

Because you get jealous easily,
and I don't want you to overthink
and hurt yourself.

secret six

There's a hidden folder on my laptop.

It has every picture of you.
Every single one.

And your voice notes.

secret seven

I'm jealous.

Of boys. Of girls.
Of anyone who gets even a tiny bit
of your attention.

secret eight

You told me not to stay awake
after 2:30am if I'm working.

I'm not working.
I'm just staring at your pictures.

And you're in my dreams
every single night now.

secret nine

You are the first person
I mention in my prayers.

And you will be the last.

secret ten

No matter what happens,
I am not letting you go.

I changed myself for you.

And I will always love you.

secret eleven

You turned an irresponsible boy
into a responsible man.

You showed me how vulnerable I am,
and how much a person can be valued.

secret twelve

I used to want to be alone.

Now I just want you.

Ready to take any risk.
Ready to take any blame.

But the thought of losing you...
It breaks me. It makes me cry.

These are my secrets.

Now you know.

Only you.

I've never been this honest.

Thank you for being the person
I can be honest with.

I love you, Warda. 🤍
