12  ·  May

on this day

I don't just celebrate
your birthday.

I celebrate the girl who walked into my life
and made it warmer without even trying.

You're the first thing on my mind in the morning.

The last thought I hold before I sleep.

Everything ordinary feels different
when it involves you.

what I ask for you

I pray your heart always stays soft.

I pray your smile never fades.

I pray peace follows you
wherever you go.

You became my calm

without trying.

My routine, my peace, my quiet happiness.
You became all of it — silently, softly.

what happens at night

At night, when everything slows down —

my thoughts always
find their way back to you.

You live in my prayers.
You live in my thoughts.
You live in me.

for you, these words

Iss qadar tu mujhe pyaar kar

Jisey kabhi na main sakoon phir bhula…

Tu meri baahon mein duniya bhula de

I never stopped loving you quietly.

Even when I don't say it out loud —
it's always there, always you.

You make me want to be someone
who deserves you.

And that, to me, is everything.

Happy Birthday

Warda.

12 May  ·  with all my love
